Target Audience

The audience for this site will be people who work on mining near Copiapo. They travel on light vehicles thru the mountains often in the morning, the climate conditions can lead to a dangerous situation. They will know about the weather near to the mines and will take the better route.

Personas:

Cesar: Electrical Engineer, mine worker, age: 40.

Aldo Molina: Mining Engineer, mine worker, age: 28.

Scenarios:

  • Cesar needs to go work and will drive to the mine.
  • Aldo needs to move a heavy machine from a mine to other.
  • Cesar needs to move electrical equipment from Copiapo to a mine.
  • It is to late, Cesar and Aldo have to return home.
  • It is raining, Cesar and Aldo needs to know which route take.
